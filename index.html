My coffee adventure

Coffee has been a big part of my life from two years. I love everything in this domain. I like to experiment with making coffee. Each cup of coffee is different then. Of course... It depends on temperature and hardness of water, degree of beans roasting, degree of milling and few more things, but first of all depends on person who is making the coffee.

Coffee

The most important thing in coffee making are coffee beans. On the market are a lot of coffee roasters and many categories so the choose is not easy. For the first time, I advise you to try coffee from South America, because in my opinion it is easier to feel the aromas than in african coffees.

Alternatives

We can choose between a lot of coffee brewing methods like express, aeropress, chemex, drip or moka. Each person can discover ideal method for theirself. But it's like addiction if you one day choose to follow the coffee path you will never be able to come back.

Equipment

Making coffee is not only express or simple kettle. If you want to make great coffee you need to invest in some stuff. The most important things are: weight to weigh beans, mill to milling beans, water filter if you have hard water, kettle with temperature setting (it's not a must but it's really helpful) or thermometer (the cheapest way), appropriate beans and of course willingnes.
